AIIMS Delhi

All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi

About Courses Fees Facilities Gallery Contact

About AIIMS Delhi

AIIMS New Delhi is India's top medical institute established in 1956. It is known for world-class medical education, research and hospital services. It is located at Ansari Nagar, New Delhi.

Courses Offered

  • MBBS
  • B.Sc Nursing
  • M.Sc Nursing
  • MD / MS
  • DM / MCh (Super Speciality)
  • PhD
  • B.Sc Paramedical Courses

Annual Fees (Approx)

  • MBBS: ₹4,000 - ₹6,000 per year
  • B.Sc Nursing: ₹1,500 - ₹5,000 per year
  • MD/MS: ₹2,000 - ₹8,000 per year
  • Hostel Fee: ~₹2,000 - ₹6,000 per year

Note: AIIMS is one of the most affordable top institutes in India.

Facilities

  • 24x7 Hospital & Emergency Services
  • Modern Laboratories
  • Digital Library (24 hours)
  • Hostel for Boys & Girls
  • Sports Complex
  • High-tech Research Labs
  • WiFi & IT Infrastructure

About AIIMS Rishikesh

AIIMS Rishikesh was established in 2012. It is an Institute of National Importance located near the holy river Ganga in Uttarakhand. It provides world-class medical education, research and hospital facilities.

Courses Offered

  • MBBS
  • B.Sc Nursing
  • B.Sc Paramedical Courses
  • M.Sc Nursing
  • MD / MS
  • M.Ch / DM Super Speciality
  • PhD Programs

Annual Fees (Approx)

  • MBBS: ₹5,000 - ₹6,000 per year
  • B.Sc Nursing: ₹1,400 - ₹2,500 total
  • MD/MS: ₹60,000 - ₹70,000 total
  • Hostel Fee: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 per year

Note: AIIMS Rishikesh is a government institute with very low fees.

Facilities

AIIMS Patna - Information Page

AIIMS Patna

All India Institute of Medical Sciences, Patna (Bihar)

About Courses Fees Facilities Infrastructure Gallery Contact

About AIIMS Patna

AIIMS Patna is one of the premier medical institutes in India, established in 2012 under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. It is known for high-quality medical education, low fees, and strong clinical exposure.

Courses Offered

  • MBBS
  • B.Sc Nursing (Hons)
  • B.Sc Paramedical Courses
  • MD (Medicine, Pediatrics, Pathology, etc.)
  • MS (General Surgery, Orthopedics, ENT, etc.)
  • DM / MCh Super Speciality
  • M.Sc Nursing
  • PhD Programs

Annual Fees (Approx)

  • MBBS: ₹5,000 - ₹6,000 total course fee
  • B.Sc Nursing: ₹2,000 - ₹6,000 total
  • MD/MS: ₹2,000 - ₹5,000 per year
  • DM/MCh: ₹3,000 - ₹10,000 total
  • Hostel Fee: ₹2,000 - ₹4,000 per year

Note: AIIMS Patna is highly affordable compared to private colleges.

Facilities

  • 24x7 Emergency & Trauma Center
  • Advanced Operation Theatres
  • Digital Library
  • Research Laboratories
  • Separate Boys & Girls Hostels
  • WiFi Campus
  • Sports Complex & Gym

Infrastructure

AIIMS Patna has modern infrastructure with OPD blocks, modular operation theatres, ICU units, diagnostic labs, and smart classrooms. It handles thousands of patients daily and provides strong clinical exposure to students.
